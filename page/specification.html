Display

Iphone 14 Pro

  • Super Retina XDR display
  • 6.1 inch (diagonal) all screen OLED display
  • 2556 by 1179 pixel resolution at 460 ppi

The iPhone 14 Pro display has rounded corners that follow a beautiful curved design, and these corners are within a standard rectangle. When measured as a standard rectangular shape, the screen is 6.12 inches diagonally (actual viewable area is less).

Iphone 14 Pro Max

  • Super Retina XDR display
  • 6.7 inch (diagonal) all screen OLED display
  • 2796 by 1290 pixel resolution at 460 ppi

The iPhone 14 Pro Max display has rounded corners that follow a beautiful curved design, and these corners are within a standard rectangle. When measured as a standard rectangular shape, the screen is 6.69 inches diagonally (actual viewable area is less).
